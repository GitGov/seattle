    ORDINANCE __________________  
  
    AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and  
    ordering the payment thereof.  
  
    BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:  
  
    SECTION 1.  Payment of the sum of Thirteen Million, Four Hundred  
    Fifty One Thousand, One Hundred Twenty Seven and 85/100 dollars  
    ($13,451,127.85) on Summit mechanical checks numbered, 4000708657 -  
    4000711438, plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, and  
    Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) in the amount of Fifty  
    Million, One Hundred Nine Thousand, Seven Hundred Seventy Two and  
    26/100 dollars ($50,109,772.26) are presented for ratification by the  
    City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCA  
  
    SECTION 2.  Payment of the sum of Twenty Five Million, Seven Hundred  
    Sixty Seven Thousand, Four Hundred Eighty Five and 14/100 dollars  
    ($25,767,485.14) on City General Salary Fund mechanical checks  
    numbered 50788924 - 50791450 plus manual checks, agencies checks, and  
    direct deposits numbered 320001 - 3292429 representing Gross Payrolls  
    for payroll ending date June 29, 2003 as detailed in the Payroll  
    Summary Report for claims against the City which were audited by the  
    Auditing Committee and reported by said committee to the City Council  
    on Aug 7, 2003 consistent with appropriations heretofore made for  
    such purpose from the appropriate Funds, is hereby approved.  
  
    SECTION 3.  That any act consistent with the authority and prior to  
    the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and  
    confirmed.  
  
    SECTION 4.  This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the  
    payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in  
    force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor;  
    otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law  
    under the provisions of the City Charter.  
  
    Passed by the City Council on the 11th day of Aug. 2003 and signed by  
    me in open session in authentication of its passage this Aug.11,  
    2003.
