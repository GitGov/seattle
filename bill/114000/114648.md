    ORDINANCE __________________  
  
    AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and  
    ordering the payment thereof.  
  
    BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:  
  
    SECTION 1.  Payment of the sum of Twelve Million, Eight Hundred  
    Thirty Five Thousand, Seven Hundred Sixty Eight and 42/100 dollars  
    ($12,835,768.42) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000705610 -  
    4000708656 plus manual or cancellation issues for claims, plus  
    Electronic Financial Transactions (EFT) of Twenty Seven Million,  
    Eight Hundred Eighty Nine Thousand, Five Hundred Seventy Nine and  
    67/100 dollars ($27,889,579.67) are presented for ratification by the  
    City Council per Chapter 42.23 RCA.  
  
     SECTION 2. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to  
    the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and  
    confirmed.  
  
    SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the  
    payment of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in  
    force immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor;  
    otherwise it shall take effect at the time it shall become a law  
    under the provisions of the City Charter.  
  
    Passed by the City Council on the 4h day of Aug. 2003 and signed by  
    me in open session in authentication of its passage this Aug. 4,  
    2003.
