    ORDINANCE __________________  
  
    AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and  
    ordering the payment thereof.  
  
    BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:  
  
    SECTION 1.  Payment of the sum of Eleven Million, Three Hundred Thirty  
    Nine Thousand, Five Hundred Thirty Three and 53/100 dollars  
    ($11,339,533.53) on Summit mechanical warrants numbered, 4000658430-  
    4000661158, manual or cancellation issues for claims, and electronic  
    financial transactions (EFT) in the amount of Seventeen Million, Eight  
    Hundred Five Thousand, Three Hundred Ninety Four and 94/100 dollars,  
    ($17,805,394.94) are presented for ratification by the City Council  
    per Chapter 42.23 RCA.  
  
    SECTION  2. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to  
    the effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.  
  
    SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment  
    of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force  
    immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it  
    shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the  
    provisions of the City Charter.  
  
    Passed by the City Council on the 31st day of March. 2003 and signed  
    by me in open session in authentication of its passage this March. 31,  
    2003.
