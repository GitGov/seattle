    ORDINANCE __________________  
  
    AN ORDINANCE appropriating money to pay certain audited claims and  
    ordering the payment thereof.  
  
    BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY OF SEATTLE AS FOLLOWS:  
  
    SECTION 1.  Payment of the sum of Twelve Million, Three Hundred  
    Thousand, Seventy Seven and 63/100 dollars ($12,300,077.63) on Summit  
    mechanical warrants numbered, 4000669105 - 4000671764, manual or  
    cancellation issues for claims, and electronic financial transactions  
    (EFT) in the amount of Twenty Three Million, Three Hundred Twenty  
    Three Thousand, Two Hundred Eighty Two and 85/100 dollars  
    ($23,323,282.85) are presented for ratification by the City Council  
    per Chapter 42.23 RCA.  
  
    SECTION 2. That any act consistent with the authority and prior to the  
    effective date of this ordinance is hereby ratified and confirmed.  
  
    SECTION 3. This ordinance, being an ordinance required for the payment  
    of salaries or current expenses, shall take effect and be in force  
    immediately upon its approval, if approved by the Mayor; otherwise it  
    shall take effect at the time it shall become a law under the  
    provisions of the City Charter.  
  
    Passed by the City Council on the 28th day of Apr. 2003 and signed by  
    me in open session in authentication of its passage this Apr. 28,  
    2003.
